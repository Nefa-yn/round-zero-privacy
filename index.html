Privacy Policy for Round Zero

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

Introduction

Round Zero ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our boxing timer application handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Round Zero is designed to work entirely on your device without collecting, storing, or transmitting any personal data.

Data Storage

All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using your device's local storage. This includes:

  • Number of rounds
  • Round duration
  • Rest duration
  • Warning time settings
  • Sound preferences
  • Metronome settings
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Round Zero does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Audio and Sounds

The app plays audio sounds (bells, warnings, metronome) locally on your device. These sounds are stored within the app and do not require internet access. No audio data is recorded, collected, or transmitted.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Audio Playback: To play timer sounds, bells, warnings, and metronome beats during your workout
  • Background Audio: To continue playing sounds when the app is in the background or screen is locked

We do not access your microphone or record any audio.

Children's Privacy

Round Zero does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect any personal information from users of any age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after any changes indicates your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, disclosed, or lost.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our servers. All app data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
